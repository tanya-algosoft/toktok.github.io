Our History

The world today has become a global village, thanks to numerous technological advancements that has made this feet attainable. With all these great strides in technology advancements comes the need for proper harnessing of technology to solve problems thereby meeting the needs of organizations, individuals and society at large.

At GivOVIE, we are looking at solving the problems associated with donations in churches and non-profits. We understand the needs of these organizations and we have taken a bold step to employ the benefits of internet technology to make the donation process an enjoyable and unforgettable experience.

Onis .V. Sampson

GivOVIE was founded by Onis V. Sampson. In his own words, Onis writes: Volunteering in the financial management department of my local church for almost 2 years became rewarding because I came to understand the importance of making giving of tithes and other donations enjoyable. I became driven by the desire to roll back if not completely but limit the use of church envelopes in making donations. Virtually every member of my local church above the age of 18 has a phone and a bank account. Therefore with this background, I started thinking of how I could link up these two powerful platforms to make giving not just enjoying but worthwhile. In my thoughtful mode it became clear to me that this technology has become ubiquitous in America but to a large extent was still not very popular in Canada.

Interestingly I decided to launch a website reinforced with an application to allow users to use their mobile phones to give their tithes, offerings and other donations. This whole experience gave birth to the GivOVIE business platform.
